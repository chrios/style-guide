Colors

I chose these colors to simplify development. I can change the color scheme very easily in the css/lib/color.css file.

Accessibility

My color scheme is AAA rated on the WebAIM ratings scale with a contrast ratio 9.87:1.

I've strived to make my images have descriptive names and 'alt' text for screen readers or slow networks.
